WhatsApp's encryption process:

- Messages are encrypted on the sender's device and decrypted on the recipient's device.

- Each conversation has a unique encryption key.

- The encryption key is not stored on WhatsApp's servers.

Security features:

Best practices for WhatsApp security:

  1. Keep your phone and WhatsApp app updated.
  2. Enable two-factor authentication.
  3. Use a strong PIN or biometric lock on your device.
  4. Be cautious when clicking on links or downloading files from unknown sources.
  5. Regularly review your linked devices and remove any unfamiliar ones.
  6. Use the "View Once" feature for sensitive media.
  7. Enable security notifications to be alerted of changes to a contact's security code.
